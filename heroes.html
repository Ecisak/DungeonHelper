Heroes

The selection of heroes for your expeditions is crucial. Here you can find the strengths, skills, and scars of the brave.

Roles

We can divide heroes into five categories:

  • Frontline: Ranks 1–2 Frontliners deal a ton of damage and can tank some damage.
  • Backup Fighter: Ranks 1–2–3 Backup Fighters have less damage but are used for setup with stuns or debuffs.
  • Ranged: Ranks 3–4 Rangers are quick, accurate, and squishy, attacking from the back.
  • Shuffler: Any Rank Shufflers reposition with high crit and dodge. They work well with other shufflers.
  • Support: Ranks 3–4 Supports heal and buff allies, they have minor damage skills, and need protection.
