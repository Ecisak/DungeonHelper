Heroes

Selection of heroes for your expeditions is crucial. Here you can find the strengths, skills, and scars of the brave.

Roles

We can divide heroes into four categories:
  • Frontline: Ranks 1–2 Frontliners deal a ton of damage, mostly have attacks on the enemy frontline, they can tank some damage.
  • Backup Fighter: Ranks 1-2-3 Backup fighters have less damage, have stuns or debuffs, used for setup.
  • Ranged: Ranks 3–4 Rangers are quick and have high accuracy, mostly attack backline, they are squishy.
  • Shuffler: Any Rank Shufflers have high crit and dodge, their attacks reposition them, combine with other shufflers.
  • Support: Ranks 3–4 Supports heal and buff others, they have minor damage skills, protected them at all costs.
